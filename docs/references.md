# Resources & References

This page provides a curated list of books, articles, web resources, and video lectures on **Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM)** and the **Theory of Constraints (TOC)** for practitioners, software developers, and AI engineers.

---

## Foundational Books

- **APM (Association for Project Management). [*Senior Managers’ and Project Managers’ Guide to Critical Chain*](https://www.apm.org.uk/book-shop/senior-managers-and-project-managers-guide-to-critical-chain/).**  
  A practical guide introducing Critical Chain principles, 10 rules of flow, case studies, and implementation paths for project managers and executive leaders.

- **Goldratt, Eliyahu M. (1997). *Critical Chain*.**  
  The seminal business novel that introduced Critical Chain Project Management. Explains the human dynamics, student syndrome, Parkinson's Law, multitasking, and the shift from task safety padding to aggregated project and feeding buffers.
  
- **Goldratt, Eliyahu M. (1984). *The Goal: A Process of Ongoing Improvement*.**  
  The foundational book on the Theory of Constraints (TOC). Essential context for understanding bottlenecks, throughput, and constraint management.

- **Leach, Lawrence P. (2014). *Critical Chain Project Management* (3rd Edition). Artech House.**  
  Comprehensive textbook and practitioner guide detailing mathematical buffer sizing formulas, multi-project environments, statistical foundation of CCPM, and implementation frameworks.

- **Müller, Wolfram. [*The Book of Dolphins - Part I - (agile) Critical Chain Project Management*](https://leanpub.com/bookofdolphins-criticalchainprojectmanagement). Leanpub.**  
  Combines Agile practices with Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM) to optimize resource utilization, smooth project flow, and eliminate deadline stress.

- **Newbold, Robert C. (1998). *Project Management in the Fast Lane: Applying the Theory of Constraints*. St. Lucie Press.**  
  Focuses on pragmatic implementation of CCPM in technical and engineering organizations, detailing scheduling mechanisms and buffer management.

## Websites & Articles

- **Müller, Wolfram. [Deep Dive into Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM)](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/deep-dive-critical-chain-project-management-ccpm-wolfram-m%C3%BCller-whu8e/)**  
  An in-depth article evaluating CCPM implementation variants, resource management principles, and core scheduling mechanics.

- **[Dolphin Universe Community](https://circle.dolphinuniverse.team/)**  
  An online practitioner community focused on Theory of Constraints (TOC), Agile integration, and Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM).

- **[TOCICO (Theory of Constraints International Certification Organization)](https://www.tocico.org/)**  
  The primary global body for TOC and CCPM certification, body of knowledge papers, conference proceedings, and research articles.

- **[Goldratt Institute](https://www.goldratt.com/)**  
  Educational resources, articles, and whitepapers on Goldratt's methods and project management applications.

- **[PMI Knowledge Base: Critical Chain Project Management](https://www.pmi.org/learning/library/critical-chain-project-management-schedule-5667)**  
  Project Management Institute (PMI) comparative analyses evaluating CPM vs. CCPM in practice.

- **[APM Blog: Critical Chain: why isn't it much more common?](https://www.apm.org.uk/blog/critical-chain-why-isn-t-it-much-more-common/)**  
  An Association for Project Management (APM) analysis discussing adoption challenges, organizational cultural shifts, and practical realities of CCPM.
